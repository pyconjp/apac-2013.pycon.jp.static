Day3 : 2013/9/16 (Mon, National Holiday)¶

Doors open at 10:00 AM.

The PyCon APAC Development Sprints is the event where developers gather, and develop freely on whatever Python project they choose. You can participate in Sprint without having to participate in day 1 or Day 2 of the main PyCon APAC event. Participation is completely free, but lunch and tea are not provided.
